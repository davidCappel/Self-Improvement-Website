Monk Mode

What is It?

This is the practice of aplying monk like priciples to your every day life. Monks in the mountains, eliminate themselves from distractions and focus toward achieving peace within themselves first. Only then are monks able to do incredible feats. The same thing applies to us. To achieve our goals we have to eliminate distractions, whether sextual, situational, or self imposed. We have to focus on inner achivement before we can accomplish outter accomplishments.
